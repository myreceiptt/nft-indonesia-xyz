## BANANOW.LAND

BANANOW Land... what place is that?

Imagine there is an island where there lives a community with lips that smile like bananas. Join hands together and do crazy fun explorations in navigating the world of Web3.

### What do we do?

We have several instant services for our community, where holders have the opportunity to get free custom videos, free custom banner images, and other creative support.

Glued together by strong Web3 developers, we also provide education and assistance services so that we can all be more secure and excited to navigate the ocean of Web3.

Facilitated by reliable marketing, we are also confident to expose projects/brands/works so that they can reach a wider market.

### What have we done?

- Made free custom videos for some of our partners. 
- Give classes for our community on smart contracts, security and other Web3 technologies.
- We work with several brands to bridge them into the world of Web3 while expanding their market.

### What is our goal?

We will take time to grow. We want to enjoy our trip. So that when the building is finished, there will be a lot of stories in every corner. The community will have a high sense of belonging and come to a sustainable society.
