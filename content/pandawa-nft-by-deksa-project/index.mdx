## Ask Me On Friday

Men-temen, ikutan X Space-nya **NFT Indonesia** bersama **DEKSA Project**, yuk!

Oh, iya! Sekalian mau ngenalin program barunya **NFT Indonesia**, yaitu **Ask Me On Friday**. Untuk sementara akan rutin sebulan dua kali, di setiap hari Jumat jam 20.00. Sebenarnya yang akan dibahas dalam program **Ask Me On Friday** gak hanya proyek NFT, kebetulan saja kali ini teman-teman di **DEKSA Project** baru saja rilis **Pandawa NFT**. Ke depannya, **Ask Me On Friday** bahasannya akan bebas aja, yaitu seputar NFT, blockchain, dan Web3.

### Pandawa NFT

**Pandawa NFT** by **Deksa Project** aims to bring the epic adventures of legendary Javanese heroes into the realm of advanced technology, elevating these stories to the next level of entertainment and preserving their legacy for future generations. This is the first iteration of the grand vision of **Deksa** called **Javaverse**, where multiple stories and legends of old are revived in the metaverse through games and modern entertainment mediums.
